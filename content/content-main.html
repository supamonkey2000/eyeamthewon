Company Name Generator - Sunday, December 3rd 4:16pm

Recently, I worked on an AI to generate names for companies. I used char-rnn-tensorflow, created by Sherjil Ozair which is based off of char-rnn, created by Andrej Karpathy. The list of companies I used to train the AI was a Wikipedia article listing companies in the United States. The program is now finished, and you can access it here.
